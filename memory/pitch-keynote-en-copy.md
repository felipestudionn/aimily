## Translation decisions worth flagging

- **"asistente"** → **"assistant"** (not "co-pilot", not "AI agent"). The ES copy is plain Spanish; the EN keeps the same restraint.
- **"propone, no decide por mí"** → **"propose to me. Not decide for me."** Direct mirror; preserves the wedge that aimily is not a replacement.
- **"se mantiene viva / hiperconectada"** → **"stays alive / hyper-connected"**. Hyphenated "hyper-connected" reads cleaner in EN.
- **Block names** are translated for clarity (not kept in Spanish):
  - Dirección Creativa y Tendencias → Creative Direction & Trends
  - Estructura de Colección y Compras → Collection Structure & Buying
  - Diseño, Desarrollo y Selección → Design, Development & Selection
  - Lanzamiento, Contenido y Venta → Launch, Content & Sales
- **"Ficha Técnica"** → **"Tech Pack"**. Industry standard EN — Felipe already uses this term elsewhere in the app.
- **"Marcaje"** → **"Markdown"**. Industry standard for the price-reduction action.
- **"Brand Book"**, **"Pantone TCX"**, **"BOM"**, **"Forecast"**, **"DTC"**, **"Ecom"** stay in English in both versions.
- **"sello, ventas, margen, entrada en tienda"** — note: ES has `Vents` which appears to be a typo for `Ventas`. Translated as `Sales`.

## What to NOT translate

- `aimily` (the wordmark)
- `aimily.app` (the URL)
- `That's all.` (already English by Felipe's choice)
- Photos, colours, layout, fonts — only text changes.
