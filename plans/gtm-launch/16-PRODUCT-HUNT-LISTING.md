## First 10 commenter strategy

Identify 10 supporters in advance and ask them to comment in the first 30 minutes. Their comments should:
- Be longer than 1 sentence (PH algorithm rewards engagement depth)
- Reference a specific feature
- Ask a follow-up question (forces Felipe to reply, which compounds engagement)

Example prompt to give supporters:
```
Hi! aimily launches on PH on May 1. Would love your support — we're launching the same day The Devil Wears Prada 2 hits theaters.

If you're free at 14:00 CET / 08:00 NYC, can you upvote and leave a comment? Doesn't have to be elaborate — even something like "love the brand voice, what tier do you recommend for an independent designer?" works perfectly. The questions are what kicks the algo into gear.

Link will be live at: producthunt.com/posts/aimily

Thanks! 🙏
```
