## HTML conversion guidelines

When converting these to HTML for Resend:

- **Container width**: 600px max
- **Font**: Inter or system-ui, 16px body, 1.6 line-height
- **Colors**: body text `#282A29` (carbon), background `#FAEFE0` (crema), buttons solid `#282A29` text crema
- **Buttons**: rounded-full, padded 14px 28px, `font-weight: 600`
- **Sign-off "— That's all."**: italic, smaller, slightly muted (carbon at 70% opacity)
- **Footer**: include `aimily — Alicante, Spain` + unsubscribe link (mandatory under GDPR)
- **Logo**: top of every email, 32px height, centered, link to aimily.app
- **Mobile**: 100% width, padding 24px both sides

Resend supports React Email components. Recommended: build a single `<aimilyEmailLayout>` component and pass content as children.
