## 4 · Brand voice

- **Editorial, not techy.** Speak like Vogue Business, not like a SaaS landing page.
- **Confident, dry, Miranda-coded.** Short sentences. Final periods. No exclamation marks unless mocking.
- **Visual minimalism.** Carbon (#282A29) + Crema (#FAEFE0). Big serif headlines, generous white space.
- **No emoji** in primary copy (memes excepted). Em-dashes welcome. Lowercase product name (`aimily`).
- **Always English.** This launch is global EN-only — even though the app supports 9 languages internally.

Forbidden:
- "Game-changing", "revolutionary", "next-gen", any startup-pitch jargon
- Generic AI-bro talk ("powered by GPT", "harness the power of AI")
- Mentioning OpenAI, Anthropic, Google or any provider by name in marketing

## 9 · Legal — read carefully

**This campaign uses references to The Devil Wears Prada and its sequel as cultural commentary.** Felipe (founder) has accepted the residual IP risk consciously. The agency's job is to **minimize exposure** while staying in the cultural conversation.

**DO**:
- Use textual references ("That's all.", "Florals? For spring? Groundbreaking.") in our own typography and visual style
- Engage with the official DWP2 social accounts via quote-tweets and replies (always native platform features, no downloading)
- Use hashtags freely: `#DevilWearsPrada2`, `#DWP2`, `#ThatsAll`, `#MerylStreep`, etc.
- Use original photography that *evokes* the aesthetic of the film (luxury offices, fashion magazine layouts) without replicating any frame

**DON'T**:
- Upload official film stills to ad campaigns (paid amplification = high-risk; organic = accepted risk)
- Use Lady Gaga imagery in any context (her legal team is the most aggressive in Hollywood)
- Use the official film poster (most-protected composition)
- Use clips from the trailer (audio Content ID = takedown in <2h)
- Imply endorsement ("As seen by Miranda Priestly", "Lady Gaga uses aimily" → do NOT)

**Bio disclaimer (required on all accounts)**: *"Not affiliated with The Devil Wears Prada, NBCUniversal, or its cast."*

If a DMCA arrives → comply in 24h. No counter-notice. No negotiation. Move to backup account if main is suspended.
