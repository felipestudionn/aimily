# Influencer Outreach — aimily Launch

**Goal**: 20 partnerships with fashion micro-influencers driving trial signups + cultural authority.
**Window**: 5 May → 31 May (post-launch, when DWP2 momentum has primed the audience)
**Budget**: €500-2,000 total (€25-100 per partnership) OR free product-only model.
**Manager**: External agency (we don't have bandwidth in-house)

---

## Selection criteria

A "good fit" influencer for aimily is:

| Criterion | Target |
|---|---|
| **Followers** | 5K-50K (true micro — bigger = generic, smaller = no reach) |
| **Vertical** | Fashion design, fashion students, emerging brands, fashion-tech, sustainable fashion, indie fashion biz |
| **Geography** | EN-speaking primarily: USA, UK, EU, Australia. Latam OK if creator is bilingual. |
| **Engagement rate** | >5% (likes+comments / followers per post) — not just follower count |
| **Content style** | Educational, behind-the-scenes, tutorial, opinion. NOT pure outfit photography. |
| **Posting cadence** | 3-7 posts/week, active comments |
| **Existing tooling content?** | Bonus if they already cover Notion, Adobe, Canva, Figma — they're inclined to feature tools |

**Hard NOs**:
- Pure aesthetic accounts with no educational/tutorial content
- Influencers who've done >5 sponsored posts in last 30 days (audience fatigue)
- Accounts with bot-pattern engagement (engagement rate <2% with high follower count)

## Tier strategy

Split the 20 across 3 tiers:

| Tier | Count | Followers | Compensation | Asks |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **Tier 1 — Anchor** | 5 | 25K-50K | €100/post + free Professional plan (€269/mo, 6 months) | 1 dedicated post + 2 stories |
| **Tier 2 — Core** | 10 | 10K-25K | €50/post + free Starter plan (€159/mo, 3 months) | 1 dedicated post OR 3 stories |
| **Tier 3 — Discovery** | 5 | 5K-10K | Free Starter plan (€159/mo, 3 months) only | 3 stories OR 1 reel |

**Total cash**: 5 × €100 + 10 × €50 = **€1,000** + product comp (no cash cost since it's their own platform)

## Briefing pack (send AFTER they say yes)

Once an influencer agrees, send them this single-document brief:

### What aimily is (1 paragraph)
aimily is an AI-powered fashion collection assistant. From the spark of inspiration to a sold-out launch — one platform handles every step: creative direction, merchandising plan, design & development, marketing & launch. Built for independent designers and emerging fashion brands. €159/mo for the Starter plan. 14-day free trial.

### What we'd love you to highlight
- The 4-block workflow (Creative · Merchandising · Design · GTM) — pick one or two that resonate with your work
- The AI generations (moodboards, brand DNA, sketches) — show the actual quality
- The presentation deck export (the 21-slide artifact at the end is the "wow" moment)
- The DWP tribute (totally optional — if it doesn't fit your voice, ignore it)

### What we'd love you NOT to do
- Don't say "AI-generated" too many times (algorithmic penalty on some platforms)
- Don't compare us to specific competitors by name (Browzwear, Centric, etc.) — keeps it positive
- Don't claim aimily replaces actual designers — it amplifies, not replaces

### Honest disclosure (required by FTC + EU)
- Use #ad or "paid partnership with aimily" in the post (Instagram has a built-in disclosure tag)
- One disclosure per post + one per story is enough

### Hashtags
- Required: #aimilyapp #fashiontech
- Suggested: #fashiondesigner #independentdesigner #fashionAI

### Login + access
- Trial account credentials sent via 1Password share (never email)
- Test collection pre-loaded with sample data so you can demo without building from scratch
- Felipe (`hello@aimily.app`) available for any question, real time, during your content production

### Timeline
- Post within 14 days of trial start
- Send draft for approval BEFORE posting (we never edit content, but check for legal/factual errors)
- Tag `@aimily.app` so we can repost / amplify

## Compliance — FTC, EU, and platform-specific

**EU**: Spanish creators must use #publi or #colaboraciónpagada (in addition to #ad)
**US**: FTC requires "#ad" or platform-tagged paid partnership at the start of caption
**Instagram**: use the built-in "Paid Partnership" tag in addition to written disclosure
**TikTok**: tag as "Branded Content" in disclosure settings + use #ad

If an influencer refuses or "forgets" disclosure, **kill the post immediately**. Even if you've already paid. The FTC fines us, not them. €11K per non-compliant post in the US.
