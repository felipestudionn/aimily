### 4. The Name Story

```
"Aimily" is a portmanteau of "AI" and "Emily" — a reference to Emily Charlton,
the iconic fashion assistant from The Devil Wears Prada. Just as Emily handled
every operational detail of Miranda Priestly's fashion empire, Aimily handles
the operational complexity of fashion collection management. The name captures
both the AI technology at the platform's core and the role it plays: your
tireless, intelligent fashion assistant.

With The Devil Wears Prada 2 premiering May 1, 2026 — where Emily has evolved
from assistant to executive — the parallel is perfect: Aimily empowers designers
to evolve from doing everything manually to leading with AI at their side.
```

### 5. Problem & Solution

```
THE PROBLEM:
Independent fashion designers and emerging brands face the same challenge:
managing a collection requires the coordination of a full department —
trend research, merchandising strategy, design development, marketing,
and timeline management — but most operate with tiny teams and disconnected
tools (Excel, WhatsApp, email, Notion).

THE SOLUTION:
Aimily provides one integrated platform covering the complete collection
lifecycle. AI handles research, analysis, and content generation. A visual
timeline connects every milestone across departments. What previously
required a team of 10 can now be managed by a team of 1.
```
