## What aimily actually does (for tech-curious journalists)

aimily is structured as four interconnected workflow blocks, all sharing the same underlying **Collection Intelligence System** (CIS) — a server-side context loader that ensures every AI generation has full knowledge of the user's brand, audience, and prior decisions:

1. **Creative & Brand**: Brand DNA generation, consumer definition, moodboard synthesis, market research
2. **Merchandising**: Product family planning, pricing strategy, channel allocation, budget builder
3. **Design & Development**: AI sketch generation, colorway proposals, tech pack builder (with PDF export), prototyping pipeline, production tracking
4. **Marketing & Launch**: Content studio (E-commerce / Still Life / Editorial / Campaign images), drop calendar, GTM plan, presentation deck export (21 slides × 10 themes)

The novel architectural choice is the **CIS server-side load**: every one of the 16 AI endpoints calls a unified `loadFullContext()` function that pulls the entire user state from the database. This means generative outputs are always context-aware — generating a moodboard for "minimalist eyewear" produces different results than a moodboard for "streetwear sneakers", because the AI knows the brand DNA, the consumer profile, and the price tier.
